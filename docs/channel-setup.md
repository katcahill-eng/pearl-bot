## Pinned Messages

Post each message in its channel, then pin it (hover the message → … → Pin to channel).

---

### #mktg_bd_requests

This is your direct line to marketing for BD requests. A few things you can do right here:

• Grab logos, brand colors, or copy from Notion — just ask `@Sage`
• Get a quick copy review: `@Sage is this on-brand: [paste your copy]`
• Submit a request: `@Sage I need [what you need]`
• Check status: `@Sage where's my request?`

Type `@Sage help` to see everything she can do. Keep requests here, not in DMs, so nothing gets lost.
